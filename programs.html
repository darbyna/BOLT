Biology

In the BOLT Biology Program, students will be able to attain hands-on experience in various fields of the biological sciences. For instance, students will learn laboratory techniques in genetics, molecular biology, cell biology, ornithology, herpetology, and more. Each hands-on session will be taught by an experienced Ph.D. or leading industry professional. This is to ensure that the students are able to procure the best education and experience possible. Once students complete theBOLT Biology Program, they will receive the BOLT Biology Certificate of Completion.

BOLT Sequence Program (BSP)

The BOLT Sequence Program (BSP) will provide students with a digital animation of each biological pathway that is taught. This will ensure that students are able to have the best visual representation of the biological processes and thus have an enhanced comprehension of the topics.






Data Science

In the BOLT Data Science Program, students will advance in the lucrative field of data science. Students will have the ability to learn various coding languages such as Python, R, SQL, HTML, CSS, Javascript, and more. In addition, students will learn statistical techniques ranging from regression analyses (linear or logistic), classification analyses (PCA), hypothesis testing, and more. With the knowledge of statistics, each student will then transition to mastering machine learning with Python. Machine learning is advancing profusely across multiple industries and now is the time to train our youth. With the BOLT Data Science Program, students will ultimately be prepared for the future of STEM and also receive the BOLT Data Science Certificate of Completion.

DataCamp Full-Access

DataCamp is an external virtual program that will allow students the ability to continue to learn various coding skills, ranging from Python, R, and SQL, in the comfort of their own home. DataCamp has both a computer-based and mobile application that students can utilize. DataCamp is a powerful source for honing a plethora skills in coding. Career Development

In the BOLT Career Development Program students will have the opportunity to network with industry professionals and Ph.D.s after each BOLT session. This networking opportunity will allow students to learn more about the fields of STEM and how they can advance at an early age. Since BOLT focuses on High School students, now is the primary time for the students to begin jumpstarting their careers.
